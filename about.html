About Us

We are situated in the heart of Sydney and Melbourne CBD. Since 2008 Travelmate Money Exchange (The Travelmate Group Pty Ltd) has been providing over the counter Currency Exchange and International Money Transfer services to Australian customers. Travelmate offers the best and competitive rates for all Foreign Exchange Currency and International Money Transfers. We have best solutions available at the best price for all your money matters. We cater personal and business customers for both Money Exchange and Money Transfer. Travelmate is registered with the governing bodies in Australia for providing Foreign Exchange and International Money Transfers. Travelmate can beat any bank rate guaranteed provided that a written proof is available.

Travelmate management is professionally qualified and has more than 10 year experience in Retail industry. Travelmate has a team of experienced and dedicated staff that provides fast, efficient and reliable service in converting your foreign exchange currency notes and International Money Transfer. Travelmate accepts American Express Travellers Cheques in all of its outlets. Travelmate is conveniently located in the heart of Sydney CBD (4 Locations) and Melbourne CBD (1 Locations) which are open 7 days a week, 365 days a year from 8.30Am to 8.30Pm including weekends and Public Holidays. Travelmate offers delivery and pickup of Foreign Currency notes at your business premises in Sydney CBD, Sydney Metropolitan Areas and Melbourne CBD, Melbourne Metropolitan Areas.

*Fees and Charges are applicable for delivery and pickup of Foreign Exchange Currency notes in Sydney and Melbourne
